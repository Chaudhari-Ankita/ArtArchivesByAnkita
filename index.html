About Me

Hi! I'm Ankita Chaudhari, and I've been passionately immersed in the world of handcrafted art for over 6+ years. My creative journey began with paper crafts and homemade chocolates and has since expanded into a diverse range of mediums, including resin art, canvas painting, mandala designs, clay creations, and Mural(Wall) Art masterpieces.

Each piece I create is a labor of love, reflecting a personalized touch that captures every emotion and moment. I specialize in resin jewelry, where I blend intricate designs with personal significance, as well as in flower preservation, turning cherished blooms into lasting memories.

My approach to crafting is deeply rooted in customization. Whether it's for a special occasion, a unique gift, or a personal treat, I strive to bring your vision to life with meticulous attention to detail and a heartfelt connection to each project.

In addition to creating, I love sharing my passion through workshops and Art Exhibition where I guide others in discovering the joy of handcrafting.
